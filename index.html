About Us

JOTECH is a Dubai based company that specializes in Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) building services, operating since the year 2023. From the very beginning we adopted a unique approach towards our clients,catering to their specific needs and delivering upon their expectations. Behind the name of JOTECH stands an experienced and professional team that works together to deliver and maintain the highest standards of MEP services.

Our Mission

We are currently executing a number of projects in different premises of commercial,residential and industrial type.

Our Plan

Our highly qualified and technically competent managers,engineers and technicians,allow us to capture a sizable share of the market

Our Vision

We strives to deliver the highest standards of MEP installation, carefully paying attention to our client's ideas, collaborating in the development of the right scope of work.
