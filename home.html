About Us

DOXA HEGEMON is a Dubai based company that specializes in Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) building services, operating since the year 2016. From the very beginning we adopted a unique approach towards our clients,catering to their specific needs and delivering upon their expectations. Behind the nam eof DOXA HEGEMON stands an experienced and professional team that works together to deliver and maintain the highest standards of MEP services.
